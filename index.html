SAICHANDU

Sai Chand was an Indian folk singer and the Chairman of the Telangana State Warehouse Corporation. Saichand participated in various singing competitions during his school and college days. He also appeared in various Telugu reality TV singing shows like ‘Folk Star Dhoom Thadaka.’ On 29 June 2023, Sai passed away due to a heart attack. The day before his demise, he visited his farmhouse in Karukonda, which is located in the Nagarkurnool district of Telangana, along with his family.
